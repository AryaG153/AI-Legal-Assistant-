Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita

The principal criminal code of India, replacing the Indian Penal Code (IPC) to modernize the justice system.

Purpose

Consolidating and amending provisions relating to offences.

Common Use Cases
  • Criminal Liability
  • Public Order maintenance
  • Rights of victims
shopping_bag
Civil

Consumer Protection Act

A comprehensive law designed to protect the interests of consumers and provide for settlement of consumer disputes.

Purpose

Prevention of unfair trade practices and product liability claims.

Common Use Cases
  • E-commerce refunds
  • Deficient services
  • Misleading advertisements

Digital

Information Technology Act

The primary law in India dealing with cybercrime and electronic commerce, providing legal recognition for transactions.

Purpose

Legal framework for electronic governance and cyber security.

Common Use Cases
  • Data privacy breaches
  • Cyber stalking/fraud
  • Digital signatures
